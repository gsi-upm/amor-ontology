# AMOR ontology v1.0.1

AMOR v1.0.1 is a metadata and editorial maintenance release. It does not change
the conceptual model, ontology or namespace IRIs, classes, properties,
taxonomies, example assertions, annotation patterns, or competency-question
results established by v1.0.0.

## Changes since v1.0.0

- Added the standard Creative Commons Attribution 2.0 Generic legal text.
- Declared CC BY 2.0 in the six paper-facing normative Turtle graphs.
- Updated those six artefacts to version 1.0.1 with explicit
  `owl:priorVersion` links to 1.0.0 and publication dates of 2026-07-20.
- Updated the six WIDOCO configurations and changed the visible status to the
  neutral text `Ontology Specification`.
- Replaced unsupported claims in the AMOR introduction with a concise factual
  description.
- Corrected the AMOR-MFT polarity and polarity-intensity descriptions without
  changing their domain, range, or semantics.
- Extended automated validation for license, version lineage, generated RDF,
  generated HTML, and normative hashes.

## Verification

- All normative Turtle sources and generated RDF serializations parse offline.
- All ten competency questions retain their v1.0.0 bindings.
- Public Turtle, JSON-LD, and HTML representations were retrieved and validated
  after publication from the final tagged commit.
- The six public Turtle representations were byte-identical to the generated
  publication payload.
- The WIDOCO 1.4.25 image remains pinned to digest
  `sha256:ef0548a0522d26cc73f3c3ecdb265cbed59aab9eeaa51f18a6096acbc239a41c`.
