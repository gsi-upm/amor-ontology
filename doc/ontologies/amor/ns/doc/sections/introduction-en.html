Introduction back to ToC

The AMOR Ontology is a conceptual framework designed to model and represent moral values in a structured and interoperable format. AMOR stands for "Axiomatic Moral Ontology for Reasoning," emphasizing its foundation in ethical principles and its focus on supporting reasoning about moral dilemmas and value-based decision-making.

This ontology aims to provide researchers, ethicists, and developers with tools to represent diverse moral systems, enabling applications in areas such as artificial intelligence, ethics education, and societal decision-making processes.
